Reconnecting Detroit through freeway removal

As the state of Michigan considers spending $1.8 billion to add one lane to less than seven miles of I-94 in inner-city Detroit, the rest of us can find alternatives to such extensive investment that repairs and strengthens the city not just to mitigate, but to undo, the mistakes of the past. Despur Detroit is series of study proposal to remove two freeway spurs that bracket downtown Detroit: the Lodge and I-375.
